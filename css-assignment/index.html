Hyan Freitas

Hyan is a journalist from South Florida.

He is currently a Weekend Morning Producer at CBS Miami
(WFOR-CH4)

He graduated from the University of Miami in the spring of 2014. While there, he majored in Broadcast Journalism and International Studies with a minor in Music Business. He is now back at school to pursue a MFA in Interactive Media.

His undergrad years at The U included stints with student media. Leadership positions included News Director at WVUM 90.5FM, EP and Host of We The Students on UMTV.

He is a member of Alpha Epsilon Rho (AERho), the National Honor Society for Electronic Media.
